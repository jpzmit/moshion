Say no to pressure injuries.

Turn any wheelchair cushion into a smart IOT device with Moshion.

Designed by MIT Fellows

Reduce the risk of pressure injuries with the use of technologies.

Moshion:

Measures pressure and alarms when there is a high risk of a pressure injury.

Monitors moisture level to inform if a patient needs to be changed.

Tracks a patient's weight fluctuations and alarms of unusual spikes.

Designed with care.

We spoke with hundreds of patients, caregivers, nurses and doctors to create a product which fits their needs.

"I often forget to do pressure reliefs throughout the day"

"We're always busy and can be slow to notice incontinence immediately, which can lead to pressure injuries"

- Caregiver in a nursing home

"We need 2-3 people to reposition each patient regularly throughout the day and night. It's very laborious"

- Nurses in long-term care facilities
